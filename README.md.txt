## ğŸ“Š Dashboard Features

### 1. Geographic Analysis
Sales distribution across Indian states including Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, and Delhi to identify top-performing regions.

### 2. Category Performance
Product category breakdown showing Clothing (63%), Electronics (21%), and Furniture (17%) with donut chart visualization.

### 3. Monthly Profit Trends
Profit tracking across all months with color-coded indicators for positive and negative performance, revealing seasonal patterns with peak months in January, November, and December.

### 4. Customer Analysis
Analysis of top customers by purchase amount, customer segmentation, and revenue contribution patterns.

### 5. Payment Mode Distribution
- Cash on Delivery (COD): 44%
- UPI: 21%
- Credit Card: 12%
- Debit Card: 13%
- EMI: 10%

### 6. Sub-Category Performance
Horizontal bar chart showing performance across sub-categories including Printers, Bookcases, Sarees, Accessories, and Tables.
